Shopify Odoo Connector

Shopify Odoo Connector app lets you connect your Shopify storefront with Odoo.

Introducing Shopify Connector in version 15 community. The module helps in connecting odoo with shopify. Sale Orders, Customers and Products can be imported/exported between odoo and shopify.
